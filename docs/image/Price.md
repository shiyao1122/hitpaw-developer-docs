# Image Pricing

Image processing is billed **per image** based on the model category and the final output resolution (total megapixel count).

### Standard Models
**Applicable Models:** *Face Clear, Face Natural, General Enhance, High Fidelity, Sharp Denoise, Detail Denoise*

| Quality Tier | Pixel Count Range | Reference Spec | Price (Credits) | Price (USD) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base HD** | < 8 MP | 1080P / 2K / 4K | **10** | $0.01 |
| **Ultra HD** | 8 - 12 MP | 4K+ | **20** | $0.02 |
| **Extreme HD** | 12 - 24 MP | 5K / 6K | **30** | $0.03 |
| **Cinema** | 24 - 48 MP | 8K | **70** | $0.07 |
| **Large Format L1** | 48 - 108 MP | 12K | **140** | $0.14 |
| **Large Format L2** | 108 - 216 MP | 16K | **240** | $0.24 |
| **Poster** | > 216 MP | > 16K | **480** | $0.48 |
